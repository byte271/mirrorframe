FRAMEGATE

Same-origin embed

This iframe is same-origin and technically readable, but iframes are an embedding medium Mirrorframe does not reconstruct — expected skip reason: out-of-scope-medium, region masked.

Origin-isolated embed

This iframe is sandboxed without allow-same-origin, giving it an opaque origin: its document is exactly as inaccessible as a real cross-origin frame — expected skip reason: cross-origin-content, region masked.

Framegate is a self-authored Mirrorframe stress fixture. Both frames serve local, self-authored content.
